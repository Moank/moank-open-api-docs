participant Partner
participant Moank

group Creditor onboarding
Partner->Moank: POST: /creditor
activate Moank
note over Partner, Moank: The partner request onboarding of a new creditor
rbox over Moank: Process request
Moank-->Partner: Creditor data
note over Moank,Partner: The creditor data are not including the business terms
deactivate Moank

rbox over Moank: Manual process\nto set the terms

Partner->Moank: Get: /creditor/:id
activate Moank
Moank-->Partner: Creditor data
note over Moank,Partner: The creditor data including the business terms
deactivate Moank
end

group Debtor onboarding
Partner->Moank: POST: /decision
activate Moank
note over Partner, Moank: The partner request a credit decision to Moank
rbox over Moank: Process request
Moank-->Partner: Decision data
note over Moank,Partner: The decision data inlcuding the decision status and the terms from Moank
deactivate Moank

Partner->Moank: POST: /account
activate Moank
note over Partner, Moank: The partner request to convert a previous granted decision in an account
rbox over Moank: Process request
Moank-->Partner: Account data
note over Moank,Partner:The account data inlcuding the business terms Moank set for the debtor and their usage
deactivate Moank
end

activate Moank

Moank-->Partner: Claim data
note over Moank,Partner: The claim data processed by Moank
